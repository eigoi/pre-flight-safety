LOCATION-AWARE PRE-FLIGHT CHECK

Pre-Flight Safety Support

In addition to basic pre-flight checks, this prototype generates location-specific checks based on the surrounding environment. Bubble color represents the potential outcome class, while bubble size represents the effort and time needed before the planned flight can proceed.

Important: This research prototype does not guarantee legal flight eligibility or flight safety. Always verify regulations, airspace restrictions, permits, and approvals using official sources such as MLIT and DIPS2.0.

COMMON / LOCATION is shown as a text label on each bubble instead of by color.

Color is a research-prototype outcome classification based primarily on MLIT UAV Accident / Serious Incident criteria, with FAA AC 25.1309 severity definitions used as a supporting reference. It does not mean that an accident or serious incident will occur, and it is not an official legal classification. Bubble size separately represents the relative effort and time needed to address the item before flight.
